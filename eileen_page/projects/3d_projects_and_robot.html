Nixie Tube Clock

I designed this nixie tube clock using IN-12A nixie tubes, and I'm really proud of it.

These tubes, which were first introduced in 1955, run on 170V and have a beautiful, vintage aesthetic.

I made the pcb with Sebou (thx :)). I modeled, and printed the box and keycaps, painted and glued paper on it to. The case is closed with magnets.

I used :

  • Components: hot-swap
  • Microcontroller: raspberry pi pico
  • Switches: Kailh white switches
  • Connector: USB-c female
  • Hardware: magnets

I also programmed them.
